Spark and Shine Ghana

Website Planning Document

Site Name

Spark and Shine Ghana

This name was selected because it effectively communicates the company's purpose - to make spaces "spark and shine" through professional cleaning services. The inclusion of "Ghana" establishes the company's location and market focus in Accra, Ghana.

Site Purpose

The Spark and Shine Ghana website will serve as the primary digital presence for the company, designed to:

  • Showcase professional cleaning services for homes, offices, and organizations in Accra
  • Display transparent pricing information to help potential customers make informed decisions
  • Build trust through testimonials, company information, and professional presentation
  • Generate leads through an easy-to-use booking/contact form
  • Provide essential contact information and company details

Scenarios

These scenarios represent questions from potential customers that the website should address:

Scenario 1: Homeowner seeking regular cleaning service

Visitor Question: "I live in Accra and need regular home cleaning services. What services do you offer for residential cleaning, how much do they cost, and how can I schedule an appointment?"

Website Content to address this: Clear service descriptions, transparent pricing table, easy booking form with scheduling options.

Scenario 2: Office manager looking for commercial cleaning

Visitor Question: "Our office in Accra needs professional cleaning services. Are you reliable, do you have experience with commercial spaces, and can you provide references?"

Website Content to address this: Company background, testimonials from other businesses, portfolio of commercial projects, professional certifications if any.

Color Scheme

The selected color scheme reflects professionalism while incorporating bright, clean elements that represent the company's services:

Primary Color
#2E86AB
Secondary Color
#F5B700
Accent Color
#1A535C

Color Application:

  • Primary Color (#2E86AB): Used for headers, navigation, primary buttons, and key accents throughout the site
  • Secondary Color (#F5B700): Used for call-to-action elements, highlights, and important notices
  • Accent Color (#1A535C): Used for footer, section headings, and secondary text elements
  • Background Color (#F7F9FC): Light background for clean, airy feel
  • Text Color (#333333): Standard dark gray for body text for optimal readability

Typography

The typography selection focuses on readability and professional appearance:

  • Headings (h1, h2, h3): Arial, sans-serif - Clean, professional, and widely supported
  • Body Text: 'Segoe UI', Tahoma, Geneva, Verdana, sans-serif - Highly readable on screens with professional appearance

This limited font selection ensures fast loading times while maintaining visual hierarchy and professional appearance.
